About Grand Tools

At Grand Tools, we specialize in designing and manufacturing high-performance equipment for the demanding environments of pipe welding, fabrication, and heavy industry. Each product in our lineup is built with precision, durability, and safety at its core.

We have been working closely with field professionals to understand the evolving needs of the industry for the last three decades. This hands-on insight drives continuous innovation across our range of pipe stands, clamps, material handling equipment and fabrication tools.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions from our clients worldwide.

Our pipe clamps cover a wide range — from 1" to 48" depending on the product. The G-Core Align handles 1"–16", G-Force Gold/SS covers 2"–24", and the G-Rocker Chain goes up to 48". We also provide tailor-made solutions for non-standard sizes.
Yes! Our G-Force SS clamp is made entirely of stainless steel for food-grade, pharmaceutical, and clean-room applications. All our other clamps also feature stainless steel contact points to prevent carbon contamination on SS pipes.
Our pipe jacks range from 900 kg (G-Fold) for portable applications up to 5,000 kg (G-Quad Giant) for heavy industrial use. The G-Max series supports up to 3,000 kg and is ideal for large diameter pipes up to 36".
Absolutely! We export to 40+ countries across the Middle East, Europe, Southeast Asia, Africa, and the Americas. We handle all export documentation, packaging, and logistics. Our products meet international quality standards.
Yes — tailor-made solutions are one of our specialities. If you have unique pipe sizes, specific material requirements, or special working conditions, we can design and manufacture products specifically for your needs. Contact us with your requirements for a custom quote.
All Grand Tools products come with a Limited Lifetime Warranty against manufacturing defects. This covers materials and workmanship under normal use conditions. We stand behind every product we make.
Use our AI Product Advisor (the robot button at bottom-right) — it asks a few simple questions about your task, pipe size, and material, then recommends the best products. You can also check our Comparison Tables above or contact our consulting team directly.
We accept orders of any size — from single units for workshops to bulk orders for large projects. Bulk orders enjoy special pricing. Contact us for a customized quote based on your requirements.
Yes! Beyond manufacturing, we are consultants for pipe cutting and beveling. We can advise on the best equipment setup, workflow optimization, and tooling selection for your specific project requirements.
